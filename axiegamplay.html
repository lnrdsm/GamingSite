    Mobile Legends Axie Infinity

    • Features:

    1. Axie Infinity is a blockchain-based game in which players purchase NFTs of cute monsters and then pit them against each other in battles.

    2. Players can earn SLP tokens during gameplay and trade them for money at an exchange.

    3. A “play-to-earn” movement has emerged around the game, with players joining scholarship programs and academies.
